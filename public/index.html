Procryptix Ethereum Name Service

Procryptix uses ENS is to resolve human-readable names, like ‘myname.eth’, into machine-readable identifiers, including Ethereum addresses, Swarm and IPFS content hashes.

ENS has similar goals to DNS, the Internet’s Domain Name Service, but has significantly different architecture, due to the capabilities and constraints provided by the Ethereum blockchain.

Once a name is requested, an auction of 72 hours begins in which anyone can put a sealed bid and send the necessary funds (minimum bid is 0.01 ether), followed by a 48 hour "Reveal Period" in which bids must be revealed. Any bid that is not revealed results in the loss of its related funds. Revealing your bid requires information stored in your browser, or backed up using the 'Export Bids' option. The highest bidder becomes the registrant of the name, and the ether they bid will lock in the name and can be refunded if the name is released.

Procrypitx will do the work for you by maticulously going through the bidding process and securing your name for you for 0.1 ETH. That includes the minimum bid of 0.01 ETH, a sealed bid fee from ENS for 0.01 ETH and gas for at least 4 transactions. If you want to bid higher, it will have to be added to the 0.1 ETH fee.
