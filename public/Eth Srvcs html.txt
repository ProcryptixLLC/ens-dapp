<td><p>
ENS is the Ethereum Name Service,<br>a distributed, open, and extensible naming<br>system based on the Ethereum blockchain.</p>
<p>
The primary goal of ENS is to resolve<br>human-readable names, like ‘myname.eth’,<br>into machine-readable identifiers, including<br>Ethereum addresses, Swarm and IPFS<br>content hashes, and other identifiers. A<br>secondary purpose is to provide metadata<br>about names, such as ABIs for contracts,<br>and whois information for users.</p><p>
ENS has similar goals to DNS, the Internet’s<br>Domain Name Service, but has significantly<br>different architecture, due to the capabilities<br>and constraints provided by the Ethereum<br>blockchain. Like DNS, ENS operates on a<br>system of dot-separated hierarchial names<br>called domains, with the owner of a domain<br>having full control over the distribution of<br>subdomains.
</p></td>
